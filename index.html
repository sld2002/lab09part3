Liguo Yu
Associate Professor
Computer Sciences and Informatics
Indiana University South Bend

Dr. Yu received his PhD degree in Computer Science from Vanderbilt University in 2004. His advisor is Prof. Stephen R. Schach. He received his MS degree from Institute of Metal Research, Chinese Academy of Science in 1995 and his mater thesis advisors are Prof. Douxing Li and Prof. Hengqiang Ye. He received his BS degree in Physics from Jilin University in 1992 and his advisor is Prof. Chengxiang Zhang. Before joining IUSB, he was a visiting assistant professor at Tennessee Tech University. In Summer 2018, he was an adjunct associate professor at University of Notre Dame. His research interests include software coupling, software maintenance and software evolution, empirical software engineering, and open-source development. Here is the list of his publications.
